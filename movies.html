Thor: Ragnorak

Another hit out of the ballpark for the Marvel films! The third installment of the Thor films has our leading man show off his comedic chops. Although there's no love interest, Thor's new gang of misfits makes this film highly entertaining. Loki, always the scene stealer, proves he's just as much a leading man as Thor. If you are looking for an action film, be sure to watch Thor: Ragnorak!

Upcoming Reviews

This is the time of year where Hollywood begins to release their Oscar contender films.
Once such film which is starting to get some strong buzz is I, Tonya, releasing December 8th. This film features Oscar nominee Margot Robbie as the infamous Tonya Harding as well as Alison Janey and Bobby Carnvale.
The biggest film of the holiday season will no doubt be Star Wars: The Last Jedi coming to theaters December 15th. The cliff hanger from the Force Awakens will surely have die hard fans lining up opening night.
No holiday would be complete without a musical and the Greatest Showman is expected to deliver. With an all star cast of Hugh Jackman, Zac Efron, Zendaya and Michelle Williams, this film is expected to be an entertaining spectacle coming to theaters on December 20th.
Reviews for these films will be up shorty after the release date.
